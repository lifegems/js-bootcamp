    Questions¶

    1. Before meeting to consider recommending brothers as ministerial servants or elders, what should be done by individual elders? What are we looking for in a spiritual man?
    2. In addition to the Scriptures, why should we consider the information published by the faithful and discreet slave?
    3. Discuss the meaning and importance of each qualification. (1Ti 3:1-13; Tit 1:5-9)
    4. In what manner are elders and ministerial servants appointed by holy spirit? (Ac 20:28)
    5. While holding to a high Scriptural standard, why must overseers be reasonable when considering those who qualify to serve as elders and ministerial servants?
    6. Why is it good to be thorough in obtaining background information for brothers being recommended? (1Th 5:21; 1Ti 5:22)
    7. What kind of information should be compiled when brothers have:
      a) previously been involved in wrongdoing?
      b) been separated or unscripturally divorced?
      c) served in an appointed position in the past?
    8. Why is it good for elders to work with a recommended brother and his family in field service, visit him at home, and talk with him about his spiritual goals? (Lu 6:45)
