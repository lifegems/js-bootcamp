    Your Role in Promoting Christian Unity¶

    n/a

    Questions¶

    1. What role does Jesus play in God’s purpose to unify heaven and earth? (Eph 1:20-23)
    2. Why is unity within the Christian congregation important, and how do the following scriptures show that there would be unity among us? (Isa 2:2-4; Zec 8:23; 1Co 1:10; Re 7:9, 14, 15)
    3. What is key for a body of elders to be able to work together in unity?
    4. Use the following scriptures to show how an elder demonstrates submission to Christ’s headship. (Joh 7:16-18; Mt 24:45-47; Heb 13:17; 1Co 4:6; Ro 12:10b; Jas 1:19; Mt 11:28-30; Joh 21:15-17; 1Pe 5:1-3, 5; Jas 1:5)
    5. According to Hebrews 13:7, how can your example in contributing to unity influence the congregation, and what lessons can be learned from this?
    6. What effect does a unified body of elders have on those associated with the congregation? (1Co 14:25, 33, 40)
    7. Why is humility an important quality for elders?
    8. In what ways does having a superior attitude undermine one’s effectiveness as an elder? (Ro 14:4; 1Co 13:4, 5)
    9. What are some ways an elder could become guilty of abusing his power in the congregation?
    10. Why should an elder avoid showing favoritism?
    11. Why should overseers not talk down to publishers? (Pr 16:18; 22:4; 1Pe 5:5, 6)
    12. Why is it unloving to fail to correct fellow elders when they are not properly caring for their responsibilities? What balance is needed?
    13. What should we keep in mind when giving counsel to others?
    14. How does a liberal use of the Bible and our Christian publications when counseling have a unifying effect on the congregation and on our fellow elders? (2Ti 3:16, 17)
    15. Why should overseers be cautious when personal likes or dislikes may be a basis for counsel?
    16. How does following organizational procedures and instructions have a unifying effect on others?
    17. How does it promote unity when as elder is unselfish in caring for his responsibilities? (Ac 20:34, 35; Heb 13:17)
    18. According to Psalm 29:11 and Isaiah 60:17, what is the principal reason we can enjoy unity in the Christian congregation?
