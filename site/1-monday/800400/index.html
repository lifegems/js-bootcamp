    Questions¶

    1. How do the following scriptures indicate that Jehovah is organized? (Ps 103:20, 21; Isa 40:25, 26; Eze 1:12; Mt 24:45)
    2. How is Jehovah’s organizational ability balanced with his quality of happiness? (1Co 14:33; 1Ti 1:11)
    3. How does Paul’s first letter to the Corinthian congregation show that the Christian congregation is organized?
    4. Show how the congregations of the first century were organized theocratically.
    5. In what way is the Christian congregation today organized theocratically, and how is that possible?
    6. How can elders be both organized and happy in their own activities and in directing the work of others? (Pr 11:17; Ec 3:12, 13)
    7. While good organization helps in the smooth functioning of the congregation, what danger exists if you become preoccupied with organizational matters, procedures, and efficiency?
    8. In harmony with Romans 12:8, what should the coordinator of the body of elders cultivate in relation to his preparation for elders’ meetings?
    9. How does a coordinator go about compiling the agenda for an elders’ meeting?
    10. During elders’ meetings, what should the coordinator do so that he can preside “in real earnest”? (Ro 12:8; 1Ti 2:8)
    11. What responsibility do you have when the body assigns you to follow through on something?

    12. During elders’ meetings, what benefits result when you:
      a) do not insist that your personal viewpoint be adopted when issues are being discussed?
      b) listen carefully to the comments of other elders even when you may feel strongly about how a matter
      c) should be handled? (Pr 18:17; Ac 15:1, 2, 24, 25; Ro 12:3, 10)
      d) speak only when you have something meaningful to add on the point under discussion? (Pr 10:19)

    13. Who make up a Branch Committee and what do the committee’s responsibilities include?

    14. What responsibilities rest upon the circuit overseer as suggested by Acts 20:26-32 and 1 Timothy 4:11-16?
    15. How do elders show earnestness when it comes to the circuit overseer’s visit?
    16. How can you cooperate with the coordinator as he prepares for the visit of the circuit overseer?
